Jenna

Jenna has worked at Glow for 10 years running the front and back end of the salon. She knows almost every face that walks through the door and connects to them in more than just a client to employee way. She has studied both business and beauty growing up and always loved being able to join them together.

In 2016 Jenna took over Glow Tanning, renaming it Glow, which was a dream come true. She is now working on updating the direction of the salon starting with the services the salon offers by joining forces with master esthetician Amy Bagdasarian also known as "Glow With Amy." Together Jenna and Amy's dreams are able to come true by helping people Glow from within.

Amy

Amy, a Catherine Hinds graduate, has been in the beauty industry for over 10 years, completing more than 1,200 hours of medical esthetics. Her specialties include makeup application, brow shaping, and body waxing. She is also certified in microdermabrasion, chemical peels, spray tanning, and lash and brow tinting. With the ever evolving beauty trends, Amy is constantly continuing her education by attending classes for both makeup and skincare.

Amy has always enjoyed taking care of others and knew from a young age she wanted to help make others feel beautiful. She loves using makeup to enhance ones inner Glow and making every woman feel confident.
